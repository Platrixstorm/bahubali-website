The Beginning & The Conclusion

Synopsis

In the kingdom of Mahishmati, Shivudu grows up far from the palace, not knowing he is the prince. One day, he discovers the truth — he is the son of the warrior king Amarendra Baahubali. The story goes back in time to show Baahubali’s rise as a leader, his love for Devasena, and his rivalry with his cousin Bhallaladeva.

What begins as a love story turns into a tale of betrayal, politics, and war. The shocking twist comes when the loyal commander Kattappa kills Baahubali. The second part reveals why this happened and follows Shivudu as he fights to reclaim Mahishmati.
